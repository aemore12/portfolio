About Us

We are an intimate production group specializing in creating and capturing memories. With over five years of experience we are able to make those special moments even more special...


Centralized in Charlotte North Carolina we have had the pleasure of creating and capturing memories in other areas as well. Those areas include Birmingham, Alabama and Atlanta Georgia.


So, currently I am attending the Boot Camp at the University of North Carolina in Charlotte and so far I am loving it. I love the city, love the College, love the professors and I can not wait to see what’s next. And by the way, the MBA interview went well.


A couple of fun facts about me: - I played basketball for the Junior National team back in Europe - Before I started the boot camp I spoke 4 languages and I can’t wait to find out how many I am going to learn by the end of the program.
